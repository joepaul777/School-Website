NSS

National Service Scheme

The National Service Scheme (NSS) at Kadayiruppu GHSS encourages students to participate in community service activities and develop leadership skills. It aims to foster a sense of social responsibility, teamwork, and personal growth among the volunteers.

  • Social Awareness Campaigns
  • Annual Special Camp
  • Community Health Programs
  • Environmental Initiatives
