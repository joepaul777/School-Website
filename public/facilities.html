Our Facilities

Physics Lab

The Physics Lab is well-equipped to support hands-on learning in classical mechanics, optics, electricity, and magnetism. Students conduct experiments that enhance their understanding of physical principles.

Chemistry Lab

Our Chemistry Lab provides students with the tools and materials necessary to conduct practical experiments in organic, inorganic, and physical chemistry with a focus on safety and accuracy.

Computer Lab

The Computer Lab is outfitted with modern systems and high-speed internet access to support computer science education, digital literacy, and creative IT projects for all grades.

Botany Lab

The Botany Lab allows students to explore plant biology through hands-on experiments, specimen studies, and microscope analysis in a well-equipped, green-focused environment.

Zoology Lab

The Zoology Lab provides resources for studying animal biology, anatomy, and taxonomy, helping students understand the complexity of animal life through preserved specimens and models.

Smart Classroom

The Smart Classroom is equipped with interactive boards, projectors, and digital content to help teachers deliver engaging lessons, encouraging better understanding through visual and interactive tools.

Indoor Stadium

The school boasts a spacious indoor stadium for physical education, sports training, and tournaments. It supports a variety of indoor games and activities promoting fitness and teamwork.
