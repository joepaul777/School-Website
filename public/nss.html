NSS – National Service Scheme

Not Me But You – Youth for Community Service

The National Service Scheme (NSS) at GHSS Kadayiruppu embodies the spirit of voluntary service and social responsibility among students. Aligned with the motto “Not Me, But You,” the NSS unit fosters a sense of selflessness, patriotism, and community bonding.

The club conducts a variety of programs including cleanliness drives, health awareness camps, blood donation drives, anti-drug campaigns, and eco-friendly initiatives. Students engage in activities that build empathy, teamwork, and leadership — qualities essential for national development.

One of the key highlights of the NSS unit is the annual special camp, where students stay together for a week to participate in intensive community development programs. These camps offer a blend of physical work, interactive sessions, and cultural evenings, creating a life-changing experience.

NSS also encourages students to take up civic issues like waste management, water conservation, tree plantation, and disaster preparedness. Through collaborations with local Panchayats, hospitals, and NGOs, the students extend their impact to the wider society.

  • Social Awareness Campaigns & Surveys
  • Village Adoption & Development Programs
  • Free Medical Camps & Blood Donation
  • Leadership Training & Personality Development
  • Eco-Friendly Drives & Special 7-Day Camps
